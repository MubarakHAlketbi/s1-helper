Blackjack

Rules & Payouts

  • Objective: Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
  • Player Limit: Max 4 players + Dealer.
  • Bet Range: $10 (Min) - $1000 (Max) per round.
  • Card Values: 2-10 face value, J/Q/K = 10, Ace = 1 or 11.
  • Player Actions: Hit (take card) or Stand (keep hand).
  • Dealer Rules: Stands on 17 or higher (Implied standard rule).
  • Payouts:
    • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value on first two cards): Pays 1.5x bet (e.g., $10 bet wins $15 profit).
    • Win (Higher score than dealer / Dealer busts): Pays 1x bet (e.g., $10 bet wins $10 profit).
    • Push (Tie with dealer): Bet returned.
    • Lose (Bust / Lower score than dealer): Bet lost.

Ride the Bus (RTB)

Rules & Payouts

  • Objective: Correctly guess card outcomes across 4 stages.
  • Bet Range: $10 (Min) - $500 (Max) initial bet.
  • Gameplay: Progress through stages by guessing correctly. Incorrect guess loses entire potential winnings. Can forfeit to keep current winnings before next stage.
  • Answer Time: 6 seconds per question.
  • Stages & Multipliers (Inferred):
    • Stage 1: Red or Black? (Potential Winnings: Bet x 2)
    • Stage 2: Higher or Lower? (Potential Winnings: Bet x 4)
    • Stage 3: Inside or Outside? (Potential Winnings: Bet x 8)
    • Stage 4: Suit? (Potential Winnings: Bet x 32)
  • Note: Card ranking for Higher/Lower/Inside/Outside needs in-game confirmation (Ace high/low? Face card values?).
