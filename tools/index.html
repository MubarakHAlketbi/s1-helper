Tools & Planners

Use these tools to help manage your operations in Schedule 1. Plan your grows, track finances, manage deliveries, and keep tabs on your customer relationships.

Important: All data entered into these tools is stored locally in your web browser's localStorage. It is not saved on our server and will be lost if you clear your browser data.

Customer Tracker

Keep detailed notes on your customers, track relationship levels, preferred products/effects, quality standards, and deal cooldowns.

Go to Customer Tracker

Delivery Tracker

Manually log your incoming deliveries from suppliers. Track contents, estimated arrival times, and mark them as arrived.

Go to Delivery Tracker

Financial Tracker

Log your cash and online transactions, manually update your current balances, and maintain a history of your income and expenses.

Go to Financial Tracker

Grow Planner

Plan your grows by tracking planted seeds, locations, pot types, estimated harvest times, and watering needs based on pot modifiers.

Go to Grow Planner
